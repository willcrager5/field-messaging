## RIA Narrative Lead

**Groundwork for modern wealth management.**

Field is the intelligence layer for modern advisory firms — one unified view of every client, every account, every signal, across every system you use. Your data, your clients, your practice, finally working together. You own it. You govern it.

## Three-Audience Map

- **RIAs (lead):** Groundwork for modern wealth management. Advisor-owned intelligence layer.
- **Asset managers (network side):** Distribution intelligence built on anonymized, opted-in, transparently-governed advisor signal.
- **Wealthtechs / fintechs (infrastructure side):** BridgeFT-branded in the interim (see timeline). Developer-first wealth infrastructure.

## Governance & Pricing Model

RIAs own their data. Network participation is opt-in. Data leaving a firm is aggregated and anonymized. Asset managers and wealthtechs fund the network. RIAs who permission data receive Field at a materially lower price than a closed alternative. Legal to confirm specific differential before May 5.

## Vocabulary Rule

- **Field Platform** = the software customers pay for and run their practice on.
- **Field Network** = the broader multi-sided system (RIAs + AMs + wealthtechs + BridgeFT + data asset).
- BridgeFT endorser uses **"part of the Field Network."** RIA-facing surfaces use "Field Platform" or just "Field."
- Drop "ecosystem" from formal brand language.
