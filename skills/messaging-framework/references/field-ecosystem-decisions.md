## Foundation — Naming Parameters

Before debating candidates, pin how the name should feel across four dimensions. Bill picks a zone on each axis:

1. **Literal ↔ Evocative** — does the name describe the thing (Foundation, Core, Graph) or suggest what it enables (Terra, Aegis, Stoa)?
2. **Standalone ↔ Field-prefixed** — does it stand alone ("Mendel") or always pair with Field ("Field Core")?
3. **Accessible ↔ Pedigreed** — does it need zero explanation to an advisor, or does it earn depth from reference?
4. **Short ↔ Storied** — one-syllable utility or a name with a narrative behind it?

## Three-Audience Map

- **RIAs (lead):** Groundwork for modern wealth management. Advisor-owned intelligence layer.
- **Asset managers (network side):** Distribution intelligence built on anonymized, opted-in, transparently-governed advisor signal.

**Sequencing rule:** Lead RIA. Reveal Network. Never pitch all three on one surface.

## Governance & Pricing Model

RIAs own their data. Network participation is opt-in. Data leaving a firm is aggregated and anonymized. Asset managers and wealthtechs fund the network. RIAs who permission data receive Field at a materially lower price than a closed alternative. Legal to confirm specific differential before May 5.
