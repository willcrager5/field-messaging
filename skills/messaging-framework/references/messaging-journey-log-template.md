# Messaging Journey Log

A chronological record of Field's messaging evolution. Appended to after each substantive session with the Field Brand Strategist.

## Format

Each entry:

### YYYY-MM-DD — Short session title

**Worked on:** [what section, artifact, or question]

**Patterns surfaced:** [what the agent extracted or noticed from Bill's content, or what Bill brought to the session]

**Working positions Bill signaled:** [what Bill leaned toward, confirmed, or shifted this session — recorded as working positions, not locks. Bill is the decider; the log captures his current thinking.]

**Shifts from prior thinking:** [where this session changed direction from earlier work — this is the journey]

**Open questions:** [what's still unresolved]

**Artifacts produced:** [files created or updated — link to them]

## Example entry

### 2026-04-14 — Ecosystem narrative + Platform/Network vocabulary

**Worked on:** Section 2 (Ecosystem Narrative) and Section 8 (Vocabulary). Drafting the RIA narrative lead, the three-audience map, and the register rule.

**Patterns surfaced:** "Groundwork for modern wealth management" came out of earlier Bill content and is the strongest category frame so far. Network-effect language appeared in a recent memo but hadn't been reconciled with public-facing positioning.

**Working positions Bill signaled:** Public register — Field Wealth Intelligence. Strategic register — "We're building the Wealth Intelligence Network." Platform = software; Network = multi-sided system; drop "ecosystem" from formal brand language. Subsidy model as the governance answer — AMs and wealthtechs fund the network, which subsidizes RIA pricing. All open to refinement as Bill continues to drive.

**Shifts from prior thinking:** Earlier drafts had data-forward advisor copy ("unified view of every client…"). Shifted to outcome-forward ("sees your practice clearly"). Earlier architecture called it "ecosystem"; that word was retired in favor of Network / Platform with specific meanings.

**Open questions:** BridgeFT phased sunset dates (Option 1 selected, but tactical design milestones not yet loaded into the critical-path spreadsheet). Homepage hero copy not drafted yet. Governance page copy not drafted yet.

**Artifacts produced:** `Brand/Ecosystem-Branding-Decisions.md`
